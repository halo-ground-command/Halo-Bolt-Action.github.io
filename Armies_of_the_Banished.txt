Armies of the Banishedv3.0

Army Special Rules
Brotherhood of Chaos
The Banished ranks are composed of fierce but unruly factions, constantly vying for dominance.
* Effect: Once per turn, when a Banished infantry unit is destroyed, you may choose one friendly infantry or artillery unit within 9″ of it that has not yet activated this turn and roll a die:
   * On a 4+, the chosen unit activates next. Instead of drawing a random die, draw one of your dice from the bag and issue an order as normal. If pinned, an order test is still needed, as usual.
   * If the chosen unit is also within 6″ of an officer (Or an officer itself), activate on a 3+ instead.
   * If you roll below the requirement, nothing happens instead.
Atriox’s Law
Though prone to bloodlust, Banished warriors will restrain themselves in the presence of a commanding Brute.
* Effect: Units with the Berserker special rule can ignore the compulsory Run behavior caused by failing an Order test while they are within the morale bubble of an Officer. The suppression ends immediately if the unit exits the bubble or if the officer is removed from play.




Blood Price
Banished leaders do not inspire loyalty—they enforce obedience through fear and brutality.
* Effect: When a friendly Banished Infantry or Artillery unit fails an Order test while within 6″ of a friendly Banished Officer or Bloodhandler (including FUBAR), you may choose to remove one model from the unit and then re-roll the Order test. The second result stands as normal. This rule only applies to Infantry or Artillery units of three or more models.
Ruthless Push
Banished warbands press the attack through sheer brutality, even under heavy fire.
* Effect: When a Banished Infantry or Artillery unit with one or more pin markers is issued an Advance order, it may ignore the -1 to hit penalty for moving and shooting during that activation.












FORGED IN CHAINS
This rule allows you to customize Banished infantry and artillery units to represent the different warrior castes within the Banished hierarchy. Each eligible unit may be upgraded individually — you can freely mix and match Elite, Brute, and other squad types within the same force.
Units whose profiles include “FORGED IN CHAINS” in their Special Rules section may benefit from this customization.
When selected, apply all associated rules and point costs to every model in the squad. Mixed designations are not allowed within the same unit.
GRUNTS/JACKALS
Default. No Bonuses.
BRUTES
Must be taken as Regular or Veteran.
Gain the Stubborn, Strong, Fieldcraft and Berserker special rules.
Cost: +3pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (Brute-only):
 – The squad may be given Energy Shields for +3pts per model. 

 –Replace Stubborn by Fanatics for +1pt per model.
– The NCO/Officer may be given a Gravity Hammer for +1pt (This model rolls 2 dice in close combat instead of 1) 
ELITES
Must be taken as Veteran.
Gain the Behind Enemy Lines, Fanatics, Energy Shields, Fast, Strong, Strikes First and VISR special rules.
Cost: +8pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (Elite-only):
 – The squad may be given Stealth for +1pts per model. 
– The NCO/Officer may be given a Plasma Sword for +1pt (This model rolls 2 dice in close combat instead of 1)
