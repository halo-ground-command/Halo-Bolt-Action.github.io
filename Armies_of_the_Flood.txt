Armies of the Floodv3.1

Army Special Rules
Living Nightmare
All Flood embody terror and relentless aggression.
* Effect: All Flood Infantry and Artillery units have the Fanatics special rule, and automatically pass any Morale Checks.
Corrupted Arsenal
The Flood assimilate their enemies' equipment and vehicles, turning them against their creators.
* Effect: The Flood may include vehicles from other factions' army lists. These vehicles must always be inexperienced.
Predatory Ambush
The Flood can fall into an unnatural, corpse-like stillness, only to erupt in a sudden burst of violence when prey strays too close.
* Effect: When a Flood Infantry unit ends the turn on Ambush and chooses to attempt the normal end-of-turn Fire action, roll a D6 as usual. On a 4+, the unit may either fire normally or, instead of firing, declare an Assault against a visible enemy Infantry or Artillery unit within assault range. On a 3 or less, the unit does nothing and its Ambush die is returned to the bag as normal.
Hivemind Coordination
The Flood’s collective consciousness ensures cohesion even when individual leaders fall.
* Effect: Flood Infantry and Artillery units do not suffer the –1 morale penalty for losing their NCO.
Logic Plague
Flood presence corrupts and destabilizes synthetic minds, causing unpredictable malfunctions.
* Effect: Once per turn, just before activating a Flood unit, select one unactivated enemy Automaton or Composed Being within 6" of any friendly Flood unit and roll a D6:
   * 1-3: The enemy immediately receives 2 Pin markers.
   * 4-5: The enemy suffers –1 to hit for the rest of the turn.
   * 6: The enemy must take a morale check. If failed, roll on the FUBAR table; if passed, no effect.
