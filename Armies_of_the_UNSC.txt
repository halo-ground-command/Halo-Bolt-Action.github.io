Armies of the UNSC v3.1

Army Special Rules
Desperate Measures
When humanity is pushed to the brink, its soldiers rally with unwavering determination.
* Effect: When a UNSC infantry or artillery unit is reduced to half or less of its initial starting strength (rounding up), the remaining models in that unit gain the Stubborn special rule. Units that already have Stubborn gain the Fanatics special rule instead. Units that already have Fanatics gain Energy Shields. If they already have Energy Shields, they gain Resilient instead.
Experienced Commanders
UNSC officers are trained to manage diverse forces and battlefield conditions effectively.
* Effect: When a UNSC Officer uses the ‘You men, snap to action!’ ability, they may draw and assign one additional Order Die from the bag to a friendly unit within range, following the normal rules for that ability. This increases the number of units that can be activated by one.
Spartan Leadership
Spartans inspire exceptional performance from allied troops.
* Effect: Friendly UNSC units may re-roll failed Morale checks if at least one model in the unit has line of sight to a friendly Spartan model that is not Down.
Battle-Net Intrusion
UNSC commanders use linked battle-net systems and tactical AI support to exploit brief windows of advantage before the enemy can respond.
* Effect: Once per game, at the start of any Game Turn, the UNSC player may choose one Order Die to draw from the dice bag instead of drawing randomly. If this Order Die is assigned to an Officer, it cannot be used to trigger You Men, Snap to Action! If more than one UNSC player wishes to use this rule at the same time, roll off. The winner uses this rule that turn, and the loser must wait until a future turn to use it.








FOR HUMANITY’S SURVIVAL!
This rule allows you to customize units to represent specific UNSC sub-factions. Each eligible unit may be upgraded individually — you can freely mix and match Marine, ODST, and Spartan squads within the same force.
Units whose profiles include “FOR HUMANITY’S SURVIVAL!” in their Special Rules section may benefit from this customization.
When selected, apply all associated rules and point costs to every model in the squad. Mixed designations are not allowed within the same unit.
TROOPERS
Default. No Bonuses.
MARINES
Must be taken as Regular or Veteran.
Gain the Stubborn special rule.
Cost: +1pt per model.
ODSTs
Must be taken as Veteran.
Gain the Stubborn, Behind Enemy Lines, and VISR special rules.
Cost: +2pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (ODST-only):
 – The squad can be given Strong for +1pt per model.














SPARTANS
Must be taken as Veteran.
Gain the Fanatics, Fast, Behind Enemy Lines, Strong,  Strikes First and VISR special rules.
Cost: +5pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (Spartan-only):
 – The squad can be given Energy Shields for +3pts per model.
 – The squad can be given Stealth for +1pt per model.
– The NCO/Officer may be given a Melee Weapon for +1pt (This model rolls 2 dice in close combat instead of 1)
