Armies of the Forerunnerv3.1

Army Special Rules
Enhanced Weapon Systems
Forerunner weapons exhibit unmatched precision and lethality.
* Effect: When a Forerunner Infantry or Artillery unit fires a Heavy weapon, each 6+ to hit grants +1 Penetration to that shot’s damage roll. This however has no effect on HE shots/templates.
Uphold the Mantle
Guided by an ancient charge to preserve order.
* Effect: Any Regular or Veteran Forerunner unit that passes an Order test may remove 2 Pin markers instead of 1.
Warden of Balance
Forerunner doctrine punishes overextension.
* Effect: At the beginning of each turn, if your opponent has more units on the battlefield than you, select one friendly Forerunner Infantry or Artillery unit from your army list. During this turn, that unit gains +1 to hit when shooting. This does not stack across turns and expires at the end of each turn.
Teleportation Network
Phase relays enable instantaneous redeployment.
* Effect: Each time a Forerunner Officer successfully uses You men, snap to action!, you may choose one friendly Infantry or Artillery unit with Composed Beings that is being issued an order by that Snap. The order issued to that unit cannot be Advance, Run, or Assault. A unit carrying an objective cannot be chosen. If the chosen unit is required to take an Order test (e.g., because it is pinned), resolve the test as normal:
   * Failed: The unit goes Down (or follows FUBAR if applicable) as normal and does not teleport.
   * Passed (or if no Order test is required): The unit teleports up to 12", ignoring intervening units and terrain. It may not end in impassable terrain, inside a building, outside the battlefield, or within 1" of any unit. Teleporting does not trigger enemy Ambush during this movement. After teleporting, the unit resolves its assigned order (Fire, Ambush, Down, or Rally) as normal.






Counter-Assault Protocols
Forged by centuries of war against the Flood, these combat routines allow Promethean units to read the momentum of an incoming assault and cut it apart before it reaches full force. 
* Effect: When a unit with the Counter-Assault Protocols special rule is assaulted by enemy Infantry or Artillery, the attacking unit halves its total number of attack dice, rounding up, during the first round of that close combat only.
