Armies of the Covenant v3.0

Army Special Rules
Caste System
The Covenant draws its strength from the unique abilities of its member species, each excelling in different aspects of warfare.
* Effect: When creating a Covenant army, you may select one bonus from the following list:
   * Commanding Presence: Covenant Infantry and artillery units that are within 6″ of at least one Elite unit and not within an officer’s morale bubble gain a +1 Morale bonus.
   * Cannon Fodder: Your army gets a free basic inexperienced Grunt squad unit.
   * Wall of Deflection: If the line of fire to a Covenant infantry or artillery unit without the Shield Wall or Resilient rule passes through at least one friendly Shield Wall or Resilient unit, improve whatever cover save that unit already has by +1. This bonus cannot improve cover saves beyond 2+, as rolls of 1 always fail.
   * Fury Unleashed: Covenant infantry units that fail their Run/Assault order test when within 9" of a Brute unit may re-roll the failed order test.
Honor in Death
The Covenant believe that death in battle brings them closer to the Great Journey.
* Effect: When a Covenant unit is destroyed, one friendly regular or veteran infantry or artillery unit within 9" of it may immediately remove 1 pin marker.
Zealotry
Covenant troops are fanatically devoted to their cause, fighting without fear of death.
* Effect: Pinned regular or veteran Covenant units have +1 morale when taking Advance or Run order tests.
Faithful When Supervised
Covenant troops fight bravely—so long as a superior is watching.
* Effect: Covenant Infantry and Artillery units with Shirkers lose that rule while they are positioned within the morale bubble of a friendly Covenant Officer. If they exit the bubble or the Officer is lost, they regain Shirkers until within range of another valid Officer. If a unit exits the Officer’s morale bubble as part of a Snap to Action activation, it cannot regain Shirkers until the Officer and all units activated through that Snap have completed their orders.
FOR THE GREAT JOURNEY!
This rule allows you to customize Covenant infantry and artillery units to represent the different warrior castes within the Covenant hierarchy. Each eligible unit may be upgraded individually — you can freely mix and match Elite, Brute, and other squad types within the same force.
Units whose profiles include “FOR THE GREAT JOURNEY!” in their Special Rules section may benefit from this customization.
When selected, apply all associated rules and point costs to every model in the squad. Mixed designations are not allowed within the same unit.
GRUNTS/JACKALS
Default. No Bonuses.
BRUTES
Must be taken as Regular or Veteran.
Gain the Stubborn, Strong, Fieldcraft and Berserker special rules.
Cost: +3pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (Brute-only):
 – The squad may be given Energy Shields for +3pts per model. 
– The NCO/Officer may be given a Gravity Hammer for +1pt (This model rolls 2 dice in close combat instead of 1) 
ELITES
Must be taken as Veteran.
Gain the Behind Enemy Lines, Fanatics, Energy Shields, Fast, Strong, Strikes First and VISR special rules.
Cost: +8pts per model.
Optional additional upgrades (Elite-only):
 – The squad may be given Stealth for +1pts per model. 
– The NCO/Officer may be given a Plasma Sword for +1pt (This model rolls 2 dice in close combat instead of 1)
